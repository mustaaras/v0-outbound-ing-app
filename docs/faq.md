## Getting Started

### How do I sign up?
Visit [outbound.ing](https://outbound.ing), click "Sign Up", and create your account. Start with our free plan (30 emails/month).

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards through Stripe. No PayPal or other payment methods at this time.

### Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll be prorated.

## Email Generation

### How does the AI email generation work?
Our system uses the most advanced LLMs to create personalized emails based on proven sales strategies. You provide recipient details, and we generate professional outreach emails customized for your industry and goals.

### What languages do you support?
- **Free/Light**: English only
- **Pro**: Any language supported by the most advanced LLMs

### Can I edit the generated emails?
Yes! Review and edit any generated content before sending. Add your signature, make personalizations, or completely rewrite sections.

### How many emails can I generate?
- **Free**: 30 emails/month
- **Light**: 300 emails/month
- **Pro**: Unlimited

## Contact Search

### How does contact search work?
We use Google Maps Places API to find businesses by location, then scrape their websites to extract contact emails. This gives you verified business contacts ready for outreach.

### What locations can I search?
Any location worldwide! Enter cities, states, countries, or specific addresses. Examples: "San Francisco, CA", "London, UK", "New York City".

### How many emails do you find per business?
- **Free/Light**: 1-2 emails per business
- **Pro**: Up to 10 emails per business (advanced scraping)

### Are the contacts verified?
We source emails directly from business websites, so they're current and verified. However, always respect local privacy laws and opt-out requests.

### Can I export my data?
Yes, contact support to export your saved contacts and email history.

### Do you integrate with other tools?
Currently, we focus on email generation and contact search. CRM integrations are planned for future releases.

### What's your uptime guarantee?
We strive for 99.9% uptime. Use Vercel's built-in monitoring for real-time status.

### Do you comply with spam laws?
We help you create compliant outreach, but you're responsible for following local laws (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, etc.). Always include unsubscribe links and respect opt-outs.

## Troubleshooting

### "Rate limit exceeded" error
You've hit your monthly limit. Wait for reset or upgrade your plan.

### No contacts found in search
Try different location formats or broader search terms. Some areas have fewer listed businesses.

### Email generation is slow
This is normal during peak hours. Pro users get priority processing.

### Payment failed
Check your card details, billing address, and try a different card. Contact support if issues persist.
