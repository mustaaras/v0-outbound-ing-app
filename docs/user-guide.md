## ğŸ“§ Email Generation

### How It Works
Outbound.ing uses the most advanced LLMs to create personalized emails based on proven sales strategies. Each email is customized for your industry, tone, and goals.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Access the Generator
- Log into your dashboard
- Click "Generate Email" in the left sidebar
- You'll see the email generation form

#### 2. Fill in Recipient Details
```
Subject: Your compelling email subject
Category: Choose from 14 industries (SaaS, Real Estate, etc.)
Recipient Name: John Smith
Recipient Title: CEO
Recipient Company: TechCorp Inc.
```

#### 3. Customize Your Email
- **Tone**: Professional, Friendly, Direct, Casual
- **Length**: Short (2-3 sentences), Medium (4-6), Long (7-10)
- **Goal**: Get a meeting, Request feedback, Start conversation
- **Language**: English (default) or any language (Pro only)

#### 4. Choose Your Strategy
- Browse 100+ proven strategies by category
- Each strategy includes a detailed prompt
- Pro users see all strategies; Free/Light see limited selection

#### 5. Generate & Review
- Click "Generate Email"
- Review the AI-generated content
- Edit if needed (signature, personalization)
- Copy to clipboard or open in your email client

### Pro Features

## ğŸ” Contact Search

### How It Works
Find businesses and extract contact emails using location-based search powered by Google Maps and web scraping.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Access Contact Search
- Click "Search Contacts" in the sidebar
- Enter a location (city, state, country)

#### 2. Search Parameters
```
Location: "San Francisco, CA" or "London, UK"
Business Type: Restaurant, Software, Consulting, etc.
```

#### 3. Review Results
- See businesses with addresses and websites
- Click any business to extract contact emails
- System scrapes up to 10 websites per business (Pro)

#### 4. Save Contacts
- Click "Save to Contacts" to store for later
- Access saved contacts from your dashboard
- Use in future email campaigns

### Usage Limits
- **Free**: 20 searches/month
- **Light**: Unlimited searches
- **Pro**: Unlimited + advanced scraping (10 websites each)
